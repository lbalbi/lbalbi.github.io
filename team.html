Laura Balbi

Fellow Researcher

I am currently a fellow researcher at LiSeDa (Lisbon Semantics Data Lab at LASIGE), integrating the EU project “KATY - Knowledge At the Tip of Your fingers: Clinical Knowledge for Humanity”. The objectives I am directly working in within the project see the conception of a Knowledge Graph that incorporates and links clinical data from kidney cancer patients with multi-domain biomedical knowledge from several ontologies and public data sources. The conception of such a multi-disciplinary clinical graph unlocks potential for integration of AI for solving prediction tasks that can aid in novelty drug development and personalized medicine. My current research work also delves into the development of knowledge-enriched neural approaches for solving PPI -based tasks.
