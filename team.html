Get to know me!

 

I am currently a first-year PhD Student researcher at LiSeDa, Lisbon Semantics Data Lab at the LASIGE Research Unit of the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Lisbon. My research interests are on Knowledge Graphs with contradictions and ambiguous entity representations and on KGRL approaches to model them explicitly. I also integrate the EU-funded project “KATY - Knowledge At the Tip of Your fingers: Clinical Knowledge for Humanity”. The objectives I have directly worked in within the project saw the conception of a Knowledge Graph that incorporates and links clinical data from kidney cancer patients with multi-domain biomedical knowledge from several ontologies and public data sources. The conception of such a multi-disciplinary clinical graph unlocked potential for integration of AI for solving prediction tasks that can aid in novelty drug development and personalized medicine.
